About me

I am Sivakumar Balasubramanian, Associate Professor in Bioengineering at the Christian Medical College Vellore. I have been with the department since August 2014. My basic degree is in Electronics and Communication Engineering, following which I completed my master's and doctoral degrees in Bioengineering, focusing primarily in rehabilitation engineering.

My main research interest in the development and validation of tools and training methods for the effective and efficient delivery of neurorehabilitation, and quantitative human analysis.

I enjoy teaching very much as it is the best way for me to learn and strengthen my basics. I am paticularly interested in teaching courses in signal processing, mechanics of movements, and human motor control and learning.
